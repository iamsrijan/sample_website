About Us
So here we are now ! Its been an amazing journey of a year and a month of togetherness. It all started on the 5th of January, 2016 when we first met via a common friend. The day I first met her I knew there was something special about her, something drawed me towards her. Could it be love? I didn't know; it just felt so right. We had a great time. Before parting ways for the day I gathered the courage to ask for her phone number and since we started texting.

So, in a week, we started seeing each other and soon realized we both have feelings for each other. Thus began our wonderful journey together. Well, the inside story does have some rough patches once in a while. We had our fights and misunderstandings sometimes, but love always sailed across all these issues and each time we sorted it out by the end of the day. Now we've reached a deeper level of understanding of one another. We stand together as one and excited about the adventure future holds for us.
